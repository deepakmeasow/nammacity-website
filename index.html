For Partners & Organizations

NammaCity is a robust mobile solution designed for municipalities, civic organizations, and event partners to foster active citizen participation. The app integrates Google Maps, Places API, and Firebase for seamless location-based reporting, event management, and real-time polling. Advanced features include admin moderation, analytics, targeted advertising, and secure authentication. NammaCity streamlines communication between citizens and authorities, enabling data-driven decision-making and efficient issue resolution. Partner with NammaCity to enhance civic engagement, promote local events, and build smarter, more connected communities.
