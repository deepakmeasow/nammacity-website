NammaCity is your all-in-one app for engaging with your local community powered by Google Maps.
Create and explore a events around you like Cultural events, Charity drives, Workshops, Protests and more.
Report civic issues directly from an interactive map, and stay updated with real-time information.
With features like easy event creation, NammaCity empowers you to connect, participate, and help improve your city. Join the movement and make your city a better place!
